Our Technology :

Tried and Trusted since 2006

In high risk patients, between 15% and 18% of readmissions are related to Patient falls in their discharge setting, the majority of which are avoidable.

PREV provides easy to use technology for clinical teams, patients and family members to identify fall risks, share and plan interventions for receiving loved ones back into the home setting. This process is made simple by PREV’s App – a mobile solution, built on tried and tested AI technology (certified by Gartner), allowing Clinical teams to efficiently capture, annotate and identify Fall Risks in the home. Preventing the fall and the subsequent readmission.
